/**
Creates a function AlgaeFunction which takes allianceColor as an input.
Creates variables for the file and predictionSheet
*/

#function AlgaeFunction
Accepts 1 argument allianceColor
Gets the spreadsheet, gets the prediction spreadsheet "game predictor".
Sets the teamMode based on the allianceColor (this is meant to turn the text from the allianceColor into a number/boolean).
Gets the data of the team specified
Makes the processor, net, and total from the pieces
Gets what type of match it is from the dropdown in the data
Limits the amount of algae to 9
Rounds the net, processor, and total
Logs the net and processor (not the total)
Determines how many game pieces were placed based on the type of match (limits processor to 2 during min processor and gets the divTotal (net+processor)and adds more for example)
Logs the net and processor again
If the gamemode is No net or No Processor, it sets the net or processor to 0 and sets the other one to total
Puts the values given from in the target location with teamMode (allianceColor)

#function AlgaeCalculator
Finds the algae for each team using the calculator (sets red to blue, blue to red and runs AlgaeFunction on red and blue)
